Why Women in Tech Matters

Women have helped shape technology throughout history, yet they remain underrepresented in many STEM careers. When women are included in technology, teams become more innovative, products better serve diverse communities, and future generations gain role models to inspire them.

The Problem

Women continue to face barriers in technology, including gender stereotypes, unequal opportunities, and limited access to mentorship. These challenges discourage many girls and women from pursuing careers in STEM, despite having the talent and passion to succeed.
